Skill Gap Insights

Experience Levels Insight

  • Experience Group 1 (0-2 years): Candidates with minimal experience are generally seeking basic foundational skills in areas like data analysis and programming.
  • Experience Group 2 (2-5 years): Aimed at professionals with early career experience, where demand is for intermediate skills like data visualization, algorithmic knowledge, and understanding of industry-standard tools.
  • Experience Group 3 (5-8 years): Requires advanced skills such as leadership in data science projects, deep learning techniques, or advanced machine learning algorithms.
  • Experience Group 4 (8+ years): Highly experienced professionals are expected to have expertise in specialized tools, advanced statistical analysis, and strategic decision-making using data.

Top Skills in Demand

  • Machine learning algorithms and libraries – Frequently required in data science and analytics roles.
  • Data analysis and visualization tools – Strong proficiency in tools such as Power BI, Tableau, and Python's data manipulation libraries (Pandas, NumPy).
  • Data quality assessment, profiling, and validation – Understanding how to improve data quality is key for data-driven roles.

Top Qualifications

  • BBA: 33.36% of professionals in the field hold a Bachelor of Business Administration.
  • BA: 33.36% of individuals in the job market hold a Bachelor of Arts degree.
  • BCA: 33.28% of job seekers have a Bachelor of Computer Applications.
